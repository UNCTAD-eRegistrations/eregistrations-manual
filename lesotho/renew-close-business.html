User Manual

Renew or Close a Business

Complete guide to renewing your Business ID, renewing trading or industrial licences, or closing (terminating) your business and licences through the OBFC online portal in the Kingdom of Lesotho.

1. Overview

The Renew or Close a Business service allows registered businesses in Lesotho to manage the lifecycle of their Business ID and licences through the OBFC online portal. This single service handles both renewal and termination (closure) of:

  • Business ID — the unique identifier for your registered business
  • Trading Licences — licences required for commercial trading activities
  • Industrial Licences — licences required for industrial and manufacturing operations
Good to know

You can renew and terminate different items in a single application. For example, you may renew your Business ID while simultaneously closing a trading licence you no longer need.

What this service covers

This service encompasses seven distinct registrations that are processed together in one application:

Registration Action Description
Renewal Business ID Renew Extend the validity of your Business ID
Renewal Trading Licences Renew Extend the validity of your trading licence(s)
Renewal Industrial Licences Renew Extend the validity of your industrial licence(s)
Termination Business ID Close Permanently close your Business ID
Termination Trading Licences Close Permanently close a trading licence
Termination Industrial Licences Close Permanently close an industrial licence

2. Renewal vs. Closure

Understanding the difference between renewal and closure is important before you begin your application.

Renewal

Renewal extends the validity period of your Business ID or licence. Choose renewal when:

  • Your Business ID or licence is approaching its expiry date
  • Your Business ID or licence has already expired and you wish to continue operating
  • You want to maintain your business registration and continue trading

Renewal period: Business IDs are renewed for 3 years. Trading and industrial licences are typically renewed for 1 year.

Warning

If your Business ID or licence has expired, penalty fees will apply in addition to the standard renewal fees. The penalty amount depends on how many days past the expiry date you are.

Closure (Termination)

Closure permanently terminates your Business ID or licence. Choose termination when:

  • You are ceasing all business operations permanently
  • You no longer need a specific trading or industrial licence
  • Your business is being dissolved
Warning

Termination is permanent. Once your Business ID or licence is terminated, you cannot reverse the action. You would need to apply for a new registration if you wish to resume operations.

Can I do both in one application?

Yes. The system allows you to select different actions for different items. For example, you may:

  • Renew your Business ID while closing an industrial licence
  • Renew one trading licence while closing another
  • Close your Business ID along with all associated licences

3. Requirements

Before starting your application, make sure you have the following:

Prerequisites

  • An OBFC user account — You must have a registered account on the OBFC portal. If you do not have one, register at services.businessregistrations.gov.ls
  • An existing Business ID — The system will automatically retrieve your registered businesses and licences linked to your account
  • Knowledge of your business details — Business name, Business ID number, sector, and activity
  • Payment method — Ability to pay fees digitally through the portal, or capacity to pay in person at the OBFC
Note

No document uploads are required for this service. The system automatically retrieves your business and licence data from the national registry.

Entity types supported

This service supports all entity types registered with OBFC, including:

  • Sole Proprietorships
  • Partnerships
  • Companies (Private and Public)
  • Other registered entity types

4. Costs & Fees

Fees for this service are calculated automatically based on the actions you select (renewal or termination) and whether any penalties apply for late renewal.

Fee categories

The system calculates fees across the following categories:

Fee Category Applies to Notes
Business ID Renewal Fee Renewal of Business ID Standard processing fee for extending Business ID validity
Business ID Penalty Fee Late renewal of Business ID Applied when Business ID has already expired
Trading Licence Renewal Fee Renewal of trading licence(s) Per licence renewal
Trading Licence Penalty Fee Late renewal of trading licence(s) Applied when licence has expired
Industrial Licence Renewal Fee Renewal of industrial licence(s) Per licence renewal
Industrial Licence Penalty Fee Late renewal of industrial licence(s) Applied when licence has expired
Business ID Termination Fee Closure of Business ID Processing fee for permanent closure
Trading Licence Termination Fee Closure of trading licence(s) Processing fee for licence termination
Industrial Licence Termination Fee Closure of industrial licence(s) Processing fee for licence termination
Penalty fees

Penalty fees are automatically calculated based on the number of days past the expiry date. The longer you wait to renew after expiry, the higher the penalty. Renew before expiry to avoid penalties. The system will alert you when your Business ID or licence is within 90 days of expiry.

Payment

Fees can be paid digitally through the portal. The exact fee amounts are displayed on the Fees summary page before you submit your application. You will see a detailed breakdown including renewal fees, penalty fees (if applicable), and total amounts per registration.

5. Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to renew or close your business, trading licences, or industrial licences.

  1. 1

    Log in to the OBFC Portal

    Visit services.businessregistrations.gov.ls and sign in with your registered email and password. If you do not have an account, click "Register" to create one.

  2. 2

    Select "Renew or Close a Business"

    From the dashboard or service catalogue, find and click the "Renew or close a business" service to begin a new application.

  3. 3

    Load Your Businesses

    Click the "List all your businesses" button. The system will automatically retrieve all businesses registered to your account from the national database. This may take a few moments.

    Tip

    If you own a company, you will also see a button "List all your licences" to load licences connected to that company entity.

  4. 4

    Review Your Businesses

    The system displays your businesses in a table showing:

    • Business Name
    • Business ID number
    • Expiry date
    • Entity type (Sole Proprietor, Company, etc.)
    • Number of days until expiry or days since expiry

    The total number of businesses is shown at the top of the section.

  5. 5

    Select Actions for Each Business

    For each business in the list, select the action you want to take:

    • Renew — tick the "Renew" checkbox to extend the validity of that Business ID
    • Close — tick the "Close" checkbox to permanently terminate that Business ID

    You can select different actions for different businesses. You may also leave businesses unchanged by not selecting either option.

    Important

    You cannot select both "Renew" and "Close" for the same business. These are mutually exclusive actions.

  6. 6

    Review Your Licences

    Below the business list, the system displays your licences in a separate table showing:

    • Licence ID number
    • Licence name / description
    • Type (Trading or Industrial)
    • Sector and Activity
    • Expiry date
    • Number of days until expiry or days since expiry

    The total number of licences is shown at the top of the section.

  7. 7

    Select Actions for Each Licence

    For each licence, select the action you want to take:

    • Renew — tick the "Renew" checkbox to extend the validity of that licence
    • Close — tick the "Close" checkbox to permanently terminate that licence
  8. 8

    Review Fees

    Click "Go to payment" to proceed to the fees summary. The system displays a detailed breakdown of all fees, including:

    • Renewal fees for each selected Business ID
    • Penalty fees for any expired items
    • Renewal fees for each selected trading licence
    • Renewal fees for each selected industrial licence
    • Termination fees for any items marked for closure
    • Total amount due

    Review the fee breakdown carefully before proceeding.

  9. 9

    Complete Payment

    Pay the total fees through the digital payment system integrated into the portal. Follow the on-screen payment instructions to complete the transaction.

  10. 10

    Confirm and Submit

    On the final page, review the acknowledgment statement, tick the confirmation checkbox, and click "Submit" to send your application for processing.

    Tip

    You will receive a confirmation with a reference number. Keep this number for future reference and to track the status of your application.

6. After Submission

Once you submit your application, it goes through a structured review and approval workflow.

Processing workflow

Application Submitted

Your application is received and assigned a reference number. You can track its status from your portal dashboard.

Day 0

Review

An OBFC officer reviews your application to verify the details and ensure all information is correct. They may approve, request changes, or send the application back to you for corrections.

Day 1–2

Approval

A senior officer approves the reviewed application. At this stage, the renewal or termination is formally authorised.

Day 2–3

System Update

The system automatically updates the national business registry. Business IDs and licences are either renewed with new expiry dates or marked as terminated.

Day 3–4

Certificates Issued

Updated Business ID certificates and renewed licences are generated and made available for download from your portal account.

Day 4–5

Workflow roles

Your application is processed through the following roles:

Role Type Responsibility
Applicant You Submit the application with selected actions and payment
Review OBFC Officer Verify application details and check compliance
Approval Senior Officer Final approval or rejection of the application
BUSINESSES update Automated Automatically updates the business registry database
LICENCES update Automated Automatically updates licence records
BUSINESS ID upload Automated Generates updated Business ID certificate
LICENCES upload Automated Generates updated licence certificates
Tracking your application

You can monitor your application status at any time by logging into the portal and checking your dashboard. You will also receive email notifications when your application status changes.

If your application is sent back

If the reviewing officer finds issues with your application, it may be sent back to you with comments. In this case:

  1. Log in to the portal and open the returned application
  2. Read the reviewer's comments carefully
  3. Make the requested corrections or provide additional information
  4. Resubmit the application

7. Frequently Asked Questions

When should I renew my Business ID?

You should renew before the expiry date to avoid penalty fees. The system will flag your Business ID when it is within 90 days of expiry, and again when it is within 60 days. You can renew at any time, even after expiry, but late renewals will incur additional penalty charges.

Can I renew a Business ID that has already expired?

Yes. You can renew an expired Business ID, but penalty fees will be added based on the number of days past the expiry date. The longer you wait, the higher the penalty.

What happens when I close (terminate) a Business ID?

Termination is permanent. Your Business ID will be marked as closed in the national registry and you will no longer be able to operate under that registration. If you wish to start a new business in the future, you will need to submit a new business registration application.

Can I renew some licences and close others?

Yes. The system allows you to select different actions for each item individually. You can renew your Business ID, renew one licence, and close another all within the same application.

What if my businesses or licences do not appear in the list?

Make sure you click the "List all your businesses" button to load your data from the registry. If they still do not appear, it may be because they are registered under a different account or entity. Contact OBFC support for assistance.

How are fees calculated?

Fees are calculated automatically by the system based on the type of action (renewal or termination), the type of registration (Business ID, trading licence, or industrial licence), and whether any penalties apply. The exact amounts are displayed on the Fees page before you submit.

Can I pay in person instead of online?

The system supports digital payment through the portal. For alternative payment arrangements, please contact the OBFC directly.

How long does processing take?

Typical processing time is 3 to 5 business days from submission to completion. This includes review, approval, and automated system updates.

Will I receive new certificates after renewal?

Yes. After approval, the system automatically generates updated Business ID certificates and licence certificates with new expiry dates. These are available for download from your portal account.

What is the difference between trading and industrial licences?

Trading licences cover commercial trading activities (buying and selling goods, retail, wholesale, etc.), while industrial licences cover manufacturing, processing, and industrial production activities. Your licence type depends on the nature of your business operations.
