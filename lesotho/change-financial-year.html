Overview

The Change Financial Year service allows registered companies in Lesotho to update their financial year-end date through the eRegistrations platform. A financial year (also known as a fiscal year) is the 12-month period your company uses for accounting and tax purposes.

Companies may need to change their financial year for several reasons: aligning with a parent company, matching seasonal business cycles, or accommodating a change in management structure. In Lesotho, companies must file annual returns and financial statements based on their financial year-end, so keeping this date accurate in the Companies Registry is important.

Who should use this service? Any registered company in Lesotho (Limited Liability Company, sole proprietorship, or other entity type) that needs to officially change the closing date of its financial year as recorded with the Companies Registry.

Before You Begin

Prerequisites

  • You must have a registered account on businessregistrations.gov.ls
  • Your company must be registered and in Active status
  • You must be listed as a director, owner, or authorised user for the company
  • You should know the new financial year-end date you wish to set

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Log in to the eRegistrations portal Visit businessregistrations.gov.ls and click the Log in button. Enter your email address and password. If you have forgotten your password, use the password recovery link on the login page.
  2. Navigate to your company dashboard After logging in, you will see a list of companies associated with your account. Click on the company whose financial year you wish to change. Make sure the company status shows Active.
  3. Select "Change Financial Year" from the services menu In the company dashboard, look for the available services. Click on Change Financial Year. The system will open the application form.
  4. Review pre-filled information The form will automatically display your company details (name, registration number, current financial year-end) pulled from the registry. Verify that this information is correct.
  5. Enter the new financial year-end date Select or enter the new date you want your financial year to end on. This date will apply going forward for all annual filing obligations.
  6. Validate the form Click the Validate the form button. The system will check that all required fields are properly filled. If there are any errors, they will be highlighted in red -- correct them before proceeding.
  7. Submit your application Once validation passes, click Validate and send to submit your request. You will receive a file number for tracking purposes.
  8. Receive confirmation After submission, you will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address. The Companies Registry will process your request and update your company records.

Required Documents

This service does not require any document uploads. The process is completed entirely through the online form. However, it is recommended that you keep a board resolution or company decision record on file for your own records.

Keep records. Although no documents are uploaded, your company should maintain internal records (such as a board resolution) authorising the change of financial year-end for audit and compliance purposes.

Practical Examples

Example 1: Aligning with the Lesotho Tax Year

Scenario: Lineo runs Lineo's Boutique (Pty) Ltd in Maseru. Her financial year currently ends on 30 June, but she wants to align with the Lesotho Revenue Authority tax year ending 31 March to simplify her tax filing.

Action: Lineo logs into businessregistrations.gov.ls, selects her company, opens the Change Financial Year service, and enters 31 March as her new financial year-end. After validation, she submits the form and receives a confirmation within 2 working days.

Example 2: New Partnership Alignment

Scenario: Thabo's construction company in Leribe has recently merged operations with a South African partner whose financial year ends 31 December. To produce consolidated statements, Thabo needs to change his company's financial year-end from 30 September to 31 December.

Action: Thabo uses the online service to submit the change. The Companies Registry approves the request, and Thabo's next annual return will be filed based on the new 31 December year-end.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my financial year more than once?
Yes, you can change your financial year-end again in the future. However, frequent changes may attract scrutiny during audits. It is advisable to choose a date that suits your business long-term.
Does this affect my tax obligations with the Lesotho Revenue Authority (LRA)?
Changing your financial year-end with the Companies Registry is a separate process from any tax registration changes. You should also notify the Lesotho Revenue Authority of the change to ensure your tax filings align with your new financial year.
What happens to the current financial year when I change the date?
If you shorten or extend the current financial year, you will need to file your annual return for the adjusted period. For example, if you change from a 30 June year-end to 31 December, you may need to file a short-period return covering 1 July to 31 December.
Who is eligible to submit this application?
Any person listed as a director, owner, signatory, or authorised user for the company on the eRegistrations portal can submit this application. If you are not listed, you will need an existing authorised person to add you first.
Is there a fee for changing the financial year?
No. This service is provided free of charge by the Lesotho Companies Registry. There are no filing or processing fees.
How long does it take for the change to be processed?
Processing typically takes 1 to 3 working days. You will receive a confirmation email once the change has been recorded in the registry.
Can I change the financial year for a company that is under liquidation?
No. This service is only available for companies with an Active status. Companies that are under liquidation, struck off, or in any non-active state cannot change their financial year.
I cannot find my company on the portal. What should I do?
Make sure you are logged in with the correct account. If your company is not listed, it may be registered under a different user. Contact the Companies Registry at the Ministry of Trade and Industry in Maseru for assistance.
