Change Financial Year

Submit a request to change your company's financial year end date with the Lesotho Companies Registry through the OBFC online platform. This guide walks you through every step of the process.

1. Overview

The Change Financial Year service allows registered companies in Lesotho to formally update the end date of their financial (accounting) year with the Companies Registry. Your financial year end date determines when your annual returns and financial statements are due.

This service is available through the One Business Facilitation Centre (OBFC) online platform, enabling you to submit your request entirely online without visiting an office in person.

Key Information

This service applies to companies already registered with the Lesotho Companies Registry. If your company is not yet registered, you must first complete the company registration process before you can change your financial year.

What is a financial year?

A financial year (also called a fiscal year or accounting year) is the 12-month period that a company uses for accounting and preparing financial statements. Common financial year end dates include:

  • 31 December — aligned with the calendar year
  • 31 March — aligned with the Lesotho Government fiscal year
  • 30 June or 30 September — used by some industries

The date you choose affects when annual returns must be filed and when financial statements are due to the Companies Registry.

2. When to Change Your Financial Year

There are several situations where a company may need or want to change its financial year end date:

  • Business restructuring — aligning the financial year with a parent company or group reporting schedule
  • Industry alignment — matching the financial year to industry-specific cycles or seasonal patterns
  • Regulatory compliance — meeting requirements from a regulator or government body that specifies a particular reporting period
  • Operational convenience — shifting the year end to a less busy period for the business
  • Merger or acquisition — harmonising financial years across merged entities
Important

Changing your financial year may affect filing deadlines for annual returns and tax obligations. Consult with your accountant or auditor before making this change to understand the implications for your financial reporting obligations.

3. Requirements

Before starting your application, ensure you have the following:

Eligibility

  • Your company must be registered with the Lesotho Companies Registry and hold an active status
  • You must be an authorised representative of the company (owner, director, signatory, or authorised user)
  • You must have a registered account on the OBFC eRegistrations platform

Information you will need

  • Company registration number — your unique registration number from the Companies Registry
  • Current financial year end date — the existing date on record with the Registry
  • New financial year end date — the date you wish to change to
  • Reason for the change — a brief explanation of why you are requesting the change

Supporting documents

You may be required to upload supporting documents. Accepted file formats are PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG, BMP, DOC, DOCX, ODT, RTF, and PAGES.

  • Board resolution or directors' resolution approving the change (if applicable)
  • Any additional supporting correspondence relevant to the change

4. Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to submit your request to change your company's financial year:

  1. 1

    Log in to the OBFC platform

    Visit services.businessregistrations.gov.ls and log in with your registered email address and password. If you do not have an account, click "Register" to create one first.

  2. 2

    Select your company

    From your dashboard, locate and select the company for which you wish to change the financial year. Ensure the company status is Active. Only companies with an active status are eligible for this service.

  3. 3

    Choose the "Change Financial Year" service

    Navigate to the list of available services for your company. Select "Change financial year" from the services list. This will open the application form.

  4. 4

    Complete the application form

    Fill in all required fields in the online form. You will need to specify:

    • The new financial year end date you wish to adopt
    • Any additional details or reasons for the change as requested

    Your personal details (full name, email address) and company information will be pre-filled from your account. Review them for accuracy.

  5. 5

    Upload supporting documents

    If required, upload any supporting documents such as a board resolution. Click the upload area and select your file, or drag and drop it into the designated zone.

    Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG, BMP, DOC, DOCX, ODT, RTF, PAGES.

  6. 6

    Validate the form

    Click the "Validate the form" button. The system will check that all required fields are complete and all information is in the correct format. If there are any errors, they will be highlighted in red — correct them and validate again.

  7. 7

    Review and submit

    Review the summary of your application to ensure all details are correct. Once satisfied, click "Validate and send" to submit your request to the Companies Registry for processing.

Screenshot: Change Financial Year application form

Tip

You can save your application as a draft and return to it later. Your progress is saved automatically as long as you do not close the browser without validating.

5. Form Fields Reference

The table below describes the key fields you will encounter in the application form:

Field Name Type Required Description
Full name Text Auto-filled Your full name as registered on the platform. Pre-filled from your account.
E-mail Email Auto-filled Your registered email address. Pre-filled from your account.
Company name Text Auto-filled The registered name of the company. Pre-filled from the selected entity.
Company registration number Text Auto-filled The unique registration number assigned by the Companies Registry.
Current financial year end Date Auto-filled The financial year end date currently on record with the Registry.
New financial year end Date Yes The new financial year end date you wish to adopt. Select a valid date using the date picker.
Reason for change Text area No A brief explanation for why you are requesting this change (optional but recommended).
Note

Fields marked as "Auto-filled" are populated automatically from your account and company records. You do not need to enter these manually. If any auto-filled information is incorrect, contact the OBFC to update your records before proceeding.

6. After Submission

Once you submit your request, the following processing steps occur:

Application received

Your request is received by the system. A confirmation notification is sent to your registered email address with a reference number.

Immediate

Document review

A Companies Registry officer reviews your application and any supporting documents for completeness and compliance.

1 – 3 business days

Decision

The officer approves or requests additional information. If approved, your company records are updated with the new financial year end date.

3 – 5 business days

Confirmation issued

You receive a confirmation notification that your financial year has been updated. The new date is reflected in your company profile on the OBFC platform.

5 – 7 business days (total)

Tracking your application

You can track the status of your application at any time by logging into your OBFC account and navigating to your application history. Your application will show one of these statuses:

  • Submitted — your application has been received and is awaiting review
  • Under review — a Registry officer is currently processing your request
  • Information requested — the officer has requested additional information or documents from you
  • Approved — your change has been approved and your records updated
  • Rejected — your request has been declined (a reason will be provided)
Important

If additional information is requested, respond promptly. Applications with outstanding information requests may be closed after 30 days of inactivity.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

The Change Financial Year service is processed through the OBFC platform. Any applicable fees will be displayed during the application process before you submit. Check the platform for the most current fee schedule.

The typical processing time is 5 to 7 business days from submission to confirmation. This may vary depending on the volume of applications and whether additional information is needed.

Yes, you can submit a new request to change your financial year. However, frequent changes may attract additional scrutiny from the Companies Registry. It is recommended that you carefully consider the new date before submitting your request.

Once the change is approved, your annual return filing deadline will be adjusted to reflect the new financial year end date. You will have the standard period after your new financial year end to file your annual return. Consult with the Companies Registry or your accountant for specific deadline calculations.

Changing your financial year with the Companies Registry does not automatically update your records with the LRA. You should notify the Lesotho Revenue Authority separately of the change and consult with your tax advisor regarding any implications for your tax return filing schedule.

The application can be submitted by the company owner, a registered director, a signatory, an accountant, an auditor, a lawyer or trustee, or any authorised user who has been granted access to the company on the OBFC platform.

If your application is rejected, you will receive a notification with the reason for rejection. Address the issues identified and submit a new application. Common reasons for rejection include incomplete documentation or selecting an invalid date. If you need clarification, contact the OBFC support team.

Once submitted, you cannot cancel the application through the online platform. If you need to withdraw your request, contact the OBFC support team directly with your application reference number.
