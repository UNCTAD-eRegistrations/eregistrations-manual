Verify Your Identity

1. Overview

The Verify Your Identity service is a mandatory Know Your Customer (KYC) process required before you can access business registration services on the Lesotho One Business Facilitation Centre (OBFC) platform. This service confirms your identity electronically using your national ID number, personal details, and a secure third-party verification system.

Identity verification helps the Government of Lesotho ensure that all business registrations are linked to verified individuals, reducing fraud and streamlining the registration process.

Good to Know

This is a free service with no fees. You only need to complete it once. After your identity is verified, your verified status carries over to all other services on the OBFC platform, including company registration, trade licensing, and tax registration.

Who Needs to Verify Their Identity?

  • Any person who wishes to register a business in Lesotho
  • Directors, shareholders, or partners of a company being registered
  • Sole proprietors applying for a trade licence
  • Anyone acting as an authorised representative for a business

What Does the Process Involve?

The identity verification process uses a secure digital system to match the information you provide against official records. The process includes:

  1. Entering your personal details (name, ID number, date of birth)
  2. Automated identity verification through a secure KYC provider
  3. Manual review by an OBFC officer (only if the automated check requires it)
  4. Confirmation of your verified status

2. Requirements

Before starting the identity verification process, make sure you have the following:

  • National Identity Document — Your Lesotho national ID card or passport. You will need your ID number.
  • Date of Birth — Your exact date of birth as it appears on your official identity document.
  • Email Address — A valid email address for your OBFC account (used for notifications and correspondence).
  • Internet-Connected Device — A computer, tablet, or smartphone with a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge).
  • OBFC Account — A registered account on the OBFC platform. If you do not have one, you can create it during the process.
Important

The first name, last name, and ID number you enter must exactly match the information on your official identity document. Any discrepancy may result in a failed verification or a manual review that takes additional time.

No Documents to Upload

This service does not require you to upload any documents. The verification is done electronically using the information you provide in the form.

3. Step-by-Step Guide

Part A: Create Your OBFC Account

If you already have an account, skip ahead to Part B.

  1. 1

    Visit the OBFC Portal

    Open your web browser and go to services.businessregistrations.gov.ls.

  2. 2

    Click "Register"

    On the login page, click the "Register" link below the login form. This will take you to the account creation page.

  3. 3

    Enter Your Account Details

    Fill in your email address, first name, last name, and choose a secure password. Make sure the name you enter here matches your official identity document, as it will be used for verification.

  4. 4

    Verify Your Email

    Check your email inbox for a confirmation message from the OBFC platform. Click the verification link to activate your account. If you do not see the email, check your spam or junk folder.

  5. 5

    Log In to Your Account

    Return to the portal and log in using your email and password.

Part B: Complete Identity Verification

Once logged in, follow these steps to verify your identity:

  1. 1

    Select "Verify Your Identity"

    From your dashboard, locate and click on the "Verify your identity" service. This will open the identity verification form.

  2. 2

    Confirm Your Personal Details

    The first section of the form shows your first name, last name, and email address as they appear in your account. Review these details carefully. If any of these are incorrect, you should update your account profile before proceeding.

  3. 3

    Enter Your ID Number

    In the second section, enter your national ID number exactly as it appears on your identity document. This is a required field.

  4. 4

    Enter Your Date of Birth

    Select your date of birth using the date picker. This must match the date on your official identity document. This is a required field.

  5. 5

    Start Identity Verification

    Click the "Start identity verification" button. The system will submit your details for automated verification. This process typically takes a few seconds.

    What Happens Behind the Scenes

    Your information is securely sent to a trusted identity verification provider. The system checks your name, ID number, and date of birth against official records to confirm your identity.

  6. 6

    Review Your Verification Status

    After the automated check completes, the form will display your verification status. There are three possible outcomes:

    • Approved — Your identity has been successfully verified. You can proceed to use other OBFC services.
    • In Review — The automated check could not fully confirm your identity. Your application has been sent to an OBFC officer for manual review. You may click "Request review" to proceed.
    • Declined — The verification was not successful. Check that the details you entered match your identity document and try again, or contact OBFC support for assistance.
  7. 7

    Close and Return to Dashboard

    Once your verification is approved (or submitted for review), click the "Close and go back to your dashboard" button to return to your main dashboard. Your verified status will be displayed on your profile.

5. What Happens After Submission

After you click "Start identity verification", your application goes through the following stages:

Automated Verification

Your personal details (name, ID number, and date of birth) are securely sent to the identity verification system for an automated check against official records.

Immediate (a few seconds)

Status Determination

The system determines your verification status. If the automated check is successful, your status is set to Approved and you can immediately proceed to use other OBFC services.

Immediate

Manual Review (If Required)

If the automated check cannot fully confirm your identity, your status is set to In Review. An OBFC officer will manually review your application. You can click "Request review" to flag your case for attention.

1-3 business days

Profile Updated

Once approved, your verified identity information (full name, gender, nationality, date of birth) is automatically stored in your profile. This information is used to pre-fill forms in other OBFC services.

Automatic upon approval

Ready for Business Registration

With your identity verified, you are now eligible to apply for business registration, trade licences, and other services available on the OBFC platform.

Ongoing

Understanding Verification Statuses

Approved

Your identity has been successfully verified by the automated system. No further action is needed from you. Your verified profile information (name, gender, nationality) has been saved and will be available when you apply for other services on the OBFC platform.

Next step: Return to your dashboard and start any business registration process.

In Review

The automated verification system was unable to fully confirm your identity. This can happen for several reasons, such as a minor mismatch in name spelling or a temporary issue with the records database. Your application has been sent to an OBFC officer for manual review.

What to do: Click the "Request review" button to notify an officer. You will be contacted if any additional information is needed. Manual reviews typically take 1-3 business days.

Declined

The verification system was unable to confirm your identity. This typically means there is a significant mismatch between the information you entered and the official records.

What to do:

  • Double-check that your first name, last name, ID number, and date of birth exactly match your identity document.
  • If you recently changed your name, ensure your identity document has been updated.
  • Try the verification process again with corrected information.
  • If the problem persists, contact OBFC support for assistance.
If Your Verification Is Declined

Do not create a new account to try again. Use the same account and re-submit the verification with corrected details. If you continue to experience issues, visit the OBFC office in person with your original identity document for in-person verification assistance.
