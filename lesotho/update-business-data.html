Update Your Business Data

Use this online service to modify registered details about your business in the Lesotho Company Registry. You can update your business name, activities, address, shareholders, managers, capital, and more — all from your computer or phone.

Online 100% Digital

Before You Start

What You Will Need

  • An account on businessregistrations.gov.ls (the same account you used to register your business)
  • Your Business ID number (assigned at original registration)
  • Updated details you wish to change (new name, new address, updated shareholder information, etc.)
  • A valid national ID document or passport for any new shareholders or managers
  • Corporate documents (if adding company shareholders)
  • Consent-to-act letters (if appointing new directors or secretaries)
  • Details of any new business activities you wish to add, including sector and activity classification
✅
Tip: Before starting your application, prepare all updated information offline. The system will read your current registered data automatically once you enter your Business ID, so you can review what is on record before making changes.

Fees and Costs

The basic modification of business data (name change, address update, shareholder changes, manager changes, capital changes) does not carry a separate government fee for the modification itself.

However, licensing fees apply when you add new activities that require a trading licence or industrial licence:

Fee TypeAmount Range (LSL)Trigger
Trading Licence M500 – M3,000 Adding an activity that requires a trading licence
Industrial Licence M500 – M4,000 Adding an activity that requires an industrial licence
Special Fee (higher tier) M5,000 – M15,000 Certain regulated activities
Special Fee (premium tier) M50,000 – M150,000 High-value regulated activities
✅
Discount: If you submit your application before 31 August of the current year, you may be eligible for a discounted licensing fee. The system calculates this automatically based on the submission date.
ℹ
Digital payment: Licensing fees can be paid digitally through the platform. The system tracks your payment status automatically.

Detailed Steps

#StepWhoWhat Happens
1 Review Modifications Registry Officer An officer reviews your submitted changes for completeness and accuracy. They verify name availability, check documents, and confirm activity classifications. If issues are found, the application is sent back to you for corrections.
2 Approve Modifications Senior Officer A senior officer gives final approval to each category of modification (name, activities, address, capital, shareholders, managers). Each category is approved individually.
3 Automated Registration System (Bot) The system automatically updates the registry databases: shareholder data, manager data, activities, business names, licences, and relations. Multiple automated bots handle different aspects of the update.
4 Certificate Upload System (Bot) An updated registration certificate is generated and uploaded to your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I update multiple things at once (e.g. name, address, and shareholders)?
Yes. When you start the application, you can tick as many modification categories as you need. The form will expand to show all relevant sections. You only need to submit one application for all the changes.
How long does the review process take?
Typical processing time is 3 to 5 working days, depending on the complexity of your modifications and current workload at the registry. Simple changes (like an address update) may be processed faster. If your application is sent back for corrections, the timeline restarts when you resubmit.
What happens if my proposed business name is already taken?
The system performs an instant availability check against both the Business Names database and the Business registry. If the name is already in use, or a similar name exists, you will be notified immediately and must choose a different name before you can proceed. There is no charge for the name check.
I am a sole proprietor. Can I modify shareholders and capital?
No. The shareholders, capital, and managers sections are only available for companies (legal persons). If you are a sole proprietor, you can modify your business name, activities, main office address, and branches.
Do I need to pay a fee to change my business address?
No. Changing your main office address or branch addresses does not incur a government fee. Fees only apply when adding new activities that require a trading or industrial licence.
Can I add a foreign shareholder or director?
Yes. The system supports both Basotho and foreign shareholders and directors. For foreign nationals, you will need to provide passport details and a foreign address. Note that certain business activities may have restrictions on foreign ownership under Lesotho law. The system will notify you if the selected activity falls under a restricted category.
What if I make a mistake after submitting?
You cannot edit the application once it is submitted. However, if the reviewing officer identifies the error, they will send the application back to you with a note. You can then make corrections and resubmit. If you notice an error before the review is complete, contact the OBFC and ask them to return the application.
How do I know my modification has been approved?
You will receive an email notification when your modification is approved. Additionally, an updated registration certificate will be generated and made available for download in your account on businessregistrations.gov.ls. You can check your application status at any time from your dashboard.
I cannot find my Business ID. Where can I get it?
Your Business ID was assigned when you first registered your business. It appears on your original registration certificate and in any official correspondence from the registry. If you cannot locate it, visit the One Stop Business Facilitation Centre (OBFC) in Maseru with a valid ID, or email the helpdesk for assistance.
Can I save my application and complete it later?
Yes. The system automatically saves your progress. You can close the browser and return later to continue filling in the form. When you log back in, navigate to your pending applications to resume where you left off.
