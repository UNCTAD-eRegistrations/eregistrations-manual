User Manual

Update Your Business Data

This guide explains how to submit a modification request to update your registered business information through the Lesotho One Business Facilitation Centre (OBFC) online platform. You can change your business name, activities, addresses, capital structure, shareholders, and managers.

1. Overview

The Update Your Business Data service allows registered businesses in Lesotho to modify their official business information held at the One Business Facilitation Centre (OBFC). Instead of visiting government offices in person, you can submit modification requests online through the eRegistrations platform.

This service covers a wide range of changes, from simple updates like a new business name or address to more complex modifications such as restructuring your share capital or changing shareholders and managers.

Who can use this service

Any business owner or authorised representative of a company registered in Lesotho can use this service. You must have an active account on the OBFC eRegistrations platform and a valid Business ID to proceed.

Note

You do not need to update all categories at once. The system allows you to select only the specific areas you wish to modify during each application.

2. What Can Be Updated

When you begin a modification request, the system will present a list of data categories. You can select one or more of the following areas to update:

Business Name

Change the registered name of your business or company. The system will check that your proposed new name is available and not already registered by another entity. You can also update business names associated with specific activities.

Business Activities

Add new business activities (by sector and activity type), close existing activities, or update business names linked to individual activities. If your new activity requires a trading licence, the system will prompt you for the relevant details.

Main Office & Branches

Update the address, district, town, telephone, and email of your main office. You can also add, modify, or remove branch (operating) offices with full contact details for each.

Capital & Share Structure

Modify your company's capital amount, update the number and value of ordinary shares, and add or change preferred share classes. The system displays your current capital alongside the proposed modifications for easy comparison.

Shareholders & Managers

Add new shareholders or managers, modify existing ones (update personal details, shareholding, or roles), or remove them from the register. For each person, you can specify whether they are a natural person or a legal entity (company/corporate).

3. Requirements

Before you begin, make sure you have the following ready:

Account & Access

  • An active account on the OBFC eRegistrations platform
  • Your Business ID (displayed in your dashboard after initial registration)
  • A computer or mobile device with internet access

Documents You May Need

  • ID Document — National ID or passport for any new or modified shareholders/managers (PDF, JPG, or PNG, max 5 MB)
  • Corporate Documents — If adding a legal entity as a shareholder, provide incorporation documents
  • Details of proposed changes — New name, address, activity codes, share amounts, etc.
Important

Ensure all information is accurate before submitting. Incorrect or incomplete applications may be sent back for corrections, which will delay processing.

4. Costs & Fees

The fees for updating business data depend on the type of modification you are making. Some changes, such as adding a new business activity, may involve a trading licence fee calculated by the system based on the activity type.

Modification Type Fee (LSL) Notes
Business name change Varies Depends on business type; name availability check is free
Add new activity Varies Licensing fee calculated automatically based on activity/sector
Close an activity Free No fee for closing an existing activity
Address update (main office / branches) Free No fee for address modifications
Capital / share structure change Varies Depends on the magnitude of the change
Shareholder / manager changes Varies Adding, modifying, or removing individuals
How fees are calculated

The system automatically calculates applicable fees based on the modifications you select. Licensing fees for new activities are determined by the sector and activity type. You will see the full breakdown before confirming your application.

5. Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to submit a business data modification request:

  1. 1

    Log In to Your Account

    Visit services.businessregistrations.gov.ls and sign in with your email and password. If you do not have an account, you must register first.

  2. 2

    Select "Update Your Business Data"

    From your dashboard, locate the Update your business data service and click on it to start a new modification request. Your Business ID and business type will be loaded automatically.

  3. 3

    Choose What to Update

    The form displays a list of checkboxes for each category of data you can modify. Tick the ones you need:

    • Name of business — change the registered business name
    • Business names (per activity) — update trading names for specific activities
    • Activities — add new activities or close existing ones
    • Main office — update your principal address and contact details
    • Branches — add, modify, or remove operating offices
    • Capital — change the capital amount and share structure
    • Shareholders — add, modify, or remove shareholders
    • Managers — add, modify, or remove managers
  4. 4

    Complete Each Modification Section

    For each category you selected, a dedicated form section will appear. Fill in the required information. The form is dynamic: fields and options appear based on your selections. See Section 6 for details on each section.

  5. 5

    Confirm Each Section

    At the end of each modification section, you must tick a confirmation checkbox (for example, "I confirm the modification of the business name"). This ensures you have reviewed the changes before proceeding.

  6. 6

    Upload Supporting Documents

    If your modifications involve new shareholders or managers, upload the required identity documents (national ID or passport) and any corporate documents for legal entities.

  7. 7

    Review and Submit

    Review all your changes one final time. Once satisfied, submit your application. You will receive a confirmation and a reference number for tracking.

Warning

You cannot edit your application after submission. Make sure all information is correct and all supporting documents are uploaded before clicking Submit.

7. After Submission

Once you submit your modification request, it goes through a review and approval workflow. Here is what to expect:

Application Submitted

Your modification request is received by the OBFC system. You will get a reference number and confirmation email.

Day 0

Review Modifications

An OBFC officer reviews your submitted modifications for completeness and accuracy. They verify documents and check that proposed names are available.

Day 1–3

Approve Modifications

A senior officer reviews and approves the validated modifications. If any issues are found, your application may be sent back for corrections.

Day 3–5

Automatic Processing

Once approved, the system automatically updates your business registration data, including shareholders, managers, activities, business names, and related records.

Day 5–6

Updated Certificate Issued

An updated business registration certificate reflecting your changes is generated and made available for download from your dashboard.

Day 6–7

Possible Outcomes

Status What It Means What to Do
File Validated Your application has passed initial review No action needed; it proceeds to approval
File Pending Additional review or information is required Check your email for requests from the reviewing officer
Send Back to Corrections Issues were found and your application has been returned Log in, review the officer's comments, correct the issues, and resubmit
File Rejected The modification request was denied Review the rejection reason, correct the issue, and submit a new request
Track your application

You can track the status of your modification request at any time by logging into your account and viewing your application history on the dashboard.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I update multiple categories at the same time?

Yes. When you start the modification request, you can select as many categories as you need (e.g., name, activities, shareholders, and address all at once). Each will have its own form section.

How do I know if a proposed business name is available?

After entering your proposed name, click the Check Availability button. The system will instantly tell you whether the name is available or already in use. If it is taken, you can try a different name.

What happens if my application is sent back for corrections?

You will receive an email notification. Log in to your account, open the returned application, review the officer's comments, make the necessary corrections, and resubmit. Your application will then re-enter the review queue.

Can I cancel a modification request after submission?

Once submitted, you cannot cancel or edit the request directly. Contact the OBFC helpline for assistance if you need to withdraw a pending application.

What file formats are accepted for document uploads?

The system accepts PDF, JPG, and PNG files. Each file should be no larger than 5 MB. Ensure documents are legible and not password-protected.

Do I need to update my shareholders if I change the capital?

If the capital change affects individual shareholdings (e.g., new shares issued), you should also update the shareholders section to reflect the new distribution. The system will validate that the total value of shares matches the declared capital.

How long does the process take?

Most modification requests are processed within 5 to 7 working days, provided all information is complete and correct. Applications that require corrections will take longer.

Is there a fee for address changes only?

No. Updating your main office address or branch addresses does not incur a fee.
